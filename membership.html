FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

IS THERE A JOINING FEE?

expand_more
No. We believe in straightforward pricing. What you see is what you pay. There are no hidden joining fees, cancellation fees, or maintenance fees.

CAN I FREEZE MY MEMBERSHIP?

expand_more
Yes, Pro and Elite members can freeze their membership for up to 3 months per calendar year at no extra cost. Basic members can freeze for a small administrative fee.

WHAT ARE THE GYM HOURS?

expand_more
We are open 24/7 for all members. Access is via a secure digital keycard provided upon sign-up. Staffed hours are from 6 AM to 10 PM daily.
